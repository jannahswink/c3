Q: Who can use the DSW?
      A: The DSW is geared toward quants, but available to all SEC users.


Q: What happens to my data when my instance is spun down?
      A: Data persists in the AWS S3 bucket linked to your username.


Q: Why are instances limited to 30 days? 
      A: The 30-day limit is a security measure to ensure instances are using the latest patches and updates.


Q: How can I find more instructions on how to use the DSW?
      A: Please check out the DSW User Guide for more detailed information.
Data Enterprise Analytics Platform (DEAP)


Q: Can I be part of the initial test group for DEAP?
      A: Please email c3projectoffice@sec.gov to submit your request.


Q: When will DEAP be available to use?
      A: Version 1 of DEAP is slated to be released August of 2017. 


Q: What key features will DEAP offer to SEC? 
      A: DEAP will provide SEC with access to an elastic data lake, as well as notebooking, jobs, visualization, and machine learning API.


Q: Will DEAP accommodate sensitive data?
      A: YES! DEAP and the DSW will be able to handle PII.
